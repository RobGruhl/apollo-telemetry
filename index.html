Reading the numbers

  • Verified visits ≈ how many kids. Only successful sessions from real browsers count — failed requests, secrets probes, headless browsers, and known bot user-agents are excluded by the query itself. Each QR scan at the table lands here.
  • The service worker hides repeat visits. After the first load, the whole site is cached on the phone, so a scout replaying the mission offline generates no new traffic. First visits are counted reliably; browsing depth is undercounted.
  • Unique devices is the raw number. Cloudflare computes it before filtering, so crawlers are in it. When the two disagree, trust verified visits.
  • This page refreshes itself every hour — a scheduled cloud agent pulls fresh Cloudflare analytics and publishes the update. The timestamps below tell you how fresh the numbers are.
